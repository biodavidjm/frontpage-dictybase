My life with Dicty

Bill Loomis


Bill Loomis has written about his interactions with Dictyostelium and those who have worked with it over the last 50 years. The stories are presented chronologically from 1965 to 2015. They are not meant to build a comprehensive history of the field, but only to pass on some interesting connections and personal reminiscences.
