Annual Meeting of the British Society for the Study of Protozoology


A special symposium on amoebas, for the annual meeting of the British Society for the Study of Protozoology (BSSP), will be held in Warwick on March 29-31, 2007. The following is the list of speakers (provisional titles indicated by hard brackets). Organized by Sandie Baldauf (University of York).
